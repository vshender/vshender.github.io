---
title: Privacy
description: "What this site collects: aggregate visit statistics via GoatCounter, no cookies, no ads, no cross-site tracking."
---

This site does not use cookies, does not show ads, and does not track you
across the web.

Visits are counted with [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com), a
privacy-friendly analytics service.  It records the page you visited, the
referring site, your browser and operating system, screen size, and country —
in aggregate, without cookies or persistent identifiers.  If you have
JavaScript disabled, a simple counting pixel is used instead.

The site is hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com), which, like
any web host, may log requests (including IP addresses) for operational
purposes; see GitHub's privacy documentation for details.
